GVW Privacy Policy:
Riders and Users

When you use the GVW Rent a Car app, you trust us with your information. We value that trust and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

I. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to users (riders/customers) of the GVW app and website. It describes:

  • What personal data we collect
  • How we use and share it
  • How we store and protect your information
  • Your rights and control over your data
II. Data Collection and Use
A. Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email
  • Booking Details: Pickup and drop locations, ride time and date
  • Payment Info: UPI, card, or wallet (processed securely via payment gateways)
  • Device Info: Device type, IP address, OS version
  • Location Data: Real-time GPS while using the app (with your permission)
B. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Confirm and manage your bookings
  • Improve customer service
  • Ensure safety and fraud prevention
  • Send trip updates, receipts, and promotional offers
C. Automated Processes

We use basic automation to:

  • Assign nearby drivers
  • Suggest offers based on ride history
  • Improve app experience based on usage patterns
D. Cookies & Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login
  • Track app performance
  • Analyze usage trends
E. Data Sharing

We may share your data:

  • With drivers to complete your ride
  • With payment processors (securely)
  • With government agencies if legally required
F. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed:

  • Booking history is stored for service and legal purposes
  • You may request data deletion anytime
III. Choice and Transparency

You have control over your data:

  • Access and update your profile anytime
  • Request to download or delete your data
  • Manage notification preferences in the app settings
IV. Legal Information
A. Data Controllers

GVW Rent a Car is responsible for managing and protecting your data.

B. Legal Basis

We collect and use your data:

  • With your consent
  • To fulfill the service you requested
  • To comply with legal obligations
C. Data Transfers

If we store data outside India, we ensure proper safeguards are in place.

D. Updates to this Policy

This policy may be updated. Users will be notified of major changes via email or in-app notification.
